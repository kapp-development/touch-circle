Mobile analysis

Answers

To help us to continuously improve and optimize the App, we use third party analytics provider Answers from Twitter, Inc. Therefore, the App will collect certain non-personal information automatically, such as your unique device identifier (UDID), mobile phone carrier and certain events in the app (appearance or content change, etc.). This information does not enable us to identify you as an individual and is for internal use only.

More about Answers and its privacy policy can be found at https://answers.io/img/privacy.pdf.

Crashlytics

To identify how and under which circumstances the App crashes, we use third party analysis service Crashlytics from Twitter, Inc. The service looks for app crashes, analyzes the collected data and provides reports to us. Therefore, Crashlytics automatically collects your device ID. This information gives an indication of how the App was functioning at the time of the crash.

More about Crashlytics and its privacy policy can be found at https://try.crashlytics.com/terms/privacy-policy.pdf.
